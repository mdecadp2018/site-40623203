Experience18

After the first nine weeks of study and practice, the Independent Study after the mid-term examination can be perfectly produced. Although many problems have occurred during the production process, the team members patiently searched and tested and helped the teachers and seniors. Next, complete one by one. And we also occurs in the course of the discussion over disagreements phenomenon, but after the next calm and time for mutual understanding, gradually discuss the most consistent with our design and production, even if the work between us to each other are not the same, in difficult times, We will help each other. Under the accumulation of bit by bit, from the first week to the eighteenth week, the knowledge learned every week is different, the semester is about to come to the end, it means that the knowledge of learning has reached a paragraph, and then To enter new courses and new knowledge, hope can be improved.

在經過前九週的學習與練習，使期中考後的專題製作能夠完美製作出來，雖然在製作過程中，發生了很多很多的問題，但經過組員們耐心地尋找和測試及老師、學長的幫助下，一一地完成。而我們在討論過程中也發生過意見不合的現象，但經過冷靜及互相理解的時間下，漸漸討論出最為符合我們的設計和製作，就算我們彼此間的工作不相同，在有困難的時候，都會互相幫助。在一點一滴的累積下，從第一週到第十八週，每週學習到的知識都不一樣，學期即將要進入尾聲了，就代表學習的知識已到一個段落了，接下來就要邁入新的課程、新的知識，希望可以越來越進步。
