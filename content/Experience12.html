Experience12

I modified the website into an English version. I also discussed with the group members the way in which they were absent. Originally, I wanted to add a new one to each person. I thought about suddenly stretching out the form and starting to discuss whether it could be presented in the form of a form. This is clear and understand. Because our form is to put pictures to indicate whether there is attendance, and the picture frame taught by the teacher at the beginning of the school is applied to the entire website, I would like to say whether the frame in the form can be removed, and then go to ask the teacher if there is any solution. Want to learn differently, you need to use different forms and different frameworks.

將網站修改成英文版本。還和組員一起討論出缺席的呈現方式，原本是想以在每個人當中新增一個出缺席，想著想著突然繃出表格，就開始討論是否能以表格的形式來呈現，這樣既清楚又明瞭。因為我們的表格是放圖片來表示是否有出席，而老師開學時教的圖片框是套用到整個網站，就想說可不可以將表格裡的框框去除，就跑去詢問老師有沒有解決辦法，學習到想要不一樣的呈現方式，就要用不同的形式、不同的框架。
